Statement of Purpose: Dairy Farming Operations

Applicant: Arwen Lorenz Llubit Fabila

Specialization: Dairy Cattle Production & Management

Training Location: Nabunturan Farmers Multipurpose Cooperative

1. Personal Journey and Motivation

My journey into the dairy industry began as a strategic decision to elevate my career profile. Encouraged by my family to seek professional opportunities abroad that offer better stability and growth, I began researching industries where diligence and skill are highly valued. After careful evaluation of overseas job markets, I chose Dairy Farming as my path.

To ensure I was fully prepared for this transition, I sought the guidance of Access Point Learning Solution in Tagum City. Through their trusted partnership network, I availed a comprehensive training package, committing myself to acquiring the hands-on skills necessary to succeed in a modern agricultural environment. This was not just a job search; it was a commitment to a new profession.

2. Professional Training and Experience

I successfully completed an intensive 720-hour On-the-Job Training (OJT) program, which ran from April 5, 2025, to July 5, 2025.

My training took place at the Nabunturan Farmers Multipurpose Cooperative, where I worked alongside a cohort of 14 colleagues. This 90-day immersion was incredibly rich in knowledge and practical experience. I did not just observe; I performed the work daily, adhering to strict schedules and standards.

3. Technical Competence and Skills Acquired

During my time at the cooperative, I mastered a wide range of operational tasks. My training covered almost every aspect of Dairy Cattle Production and Management, specifically:

  • Herd Management & Welfare: I learned proper handling procedures to ensure low-stress environments for the cattle. My responsibilities included monitoring animal health and taking specific care of heifers.
  • Feeding & Nutrition: I gained experience in preparing forage resources, implementing feeding strategies, and understanding nutritional planning to ensure optimal milk production.
  • Milking Operations: I was trained in proper milking techniques, emphasizing hygiene and the correct usage of milking equipment to maintain milk quality.
  • Sanitation & GMP: I learned to strictly follow Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). I understand the critical importance of milk handling standards and processing standards to ensure food safety.
  • Environmental Management: I learned how to utilize farm infrastructure and equipment effectively. A major part of my routine involved implementing environmental sanitation and managing waste systems across different farm sites to maintain a clean, disease-free environment.

4. Goals and Objectives

Short-term Goal: My immediate goal is to secure a position as a Dairy Farm Worker where I can apply the rich experience I gained during my training. I am eager to demonstrate my ability to work hard, follow instructions precisely, and contribute to a productive team.

Long-term Vision: I view this as a long-term career. I aim to refine my skills further, learning new technologies and management styles used internationally. Ultimately, I want to build a stable future for myself and my family while contributing to the success of the farm that employs me.

5. Conclusion

The training I completed on July 5, 2025, gave me the confidence and the capability to handle the rigors of dairy farming. I am physically fit, technically trained, and mentally prepared for the demands of this job. I am ready to bring my dedication and my operational expertise to your farm.
